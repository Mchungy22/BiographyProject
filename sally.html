Sally Skellington is a rag doll character from Disney's The Nightmare Before Christmas. She is the Pumpkin Queen of Halloween Town and has a romantic interest in Jack Skellington.

Dr. Finkelstein, a mad scientist, created Sally to serve him. Sally was kept locked away so Dr. Finkelstein could study her and control her life. Sally is different from the other monsters in Halloween Town because she was created by a resident.

Sally is always trying to get away from Dr. Finkelstein because she seeks independence and has a strong desire for respect and appreciation. She enjoys cooking, celebrating, and sewing.
