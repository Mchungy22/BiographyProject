Jack Skellington is the main character in Tim Burton's The Nightmare before Christmas. He is known as the "Pumpkin King" of Halloween Town. Jack is a tall skeletal figure with a passion for creating the best Halloween celebrations. However, he gets tired of the same old routine and decides to take over Christmas. Despite his good intentions, Jack's attempt to bring Christmas to Halloween Town leads to chaos.
