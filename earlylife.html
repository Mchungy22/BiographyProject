Background
Jack Skellington has worked his way up to Pumpkin King through his love of Halloween and passion for creating wonderful celebrations.
Family
Jack doesn't have any "living" blood-related family but the residents of Halloween Town love him like a father, brother and son. Additionally, he has a dog named Zero that is as close to family as it gets.
Early Influences
It is very clear that Jack was influenced at a young age by the enormous creativity surrounding Halloween as well as his unending curiosity.
